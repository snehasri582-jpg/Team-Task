About Our Destinations

Learn more about the iconic monuments featured on our site.

Charminar

The Charminar is a monument and mosque located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. Constructed in 1591, it is a symbol of Hyderabad and one of the most recognizable structures in India.

Ramappa Temple

The Ramappa Temple, also known as the Rudreshwara Temple, is located in Telangana, India. Built in the 13th century, it is famous for its intricate carvings and engineering marvels.

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the south bank of the Yamuna river in the Indian city of Agra. It was commissioned in 1632 by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan to house the tomb of his favorite wife, Mumtaz Mahal.
